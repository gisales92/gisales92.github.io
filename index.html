About

I'm passionate about using my creative problem-solving abilities to build efficient and highly functional web technologies to scale.

Through my experience in the publishing industry, I honed a strong attention to detail, the capacity to meet cutthroat deadlines, and an ability to work as a collaborative team member.

At App Academy, I have had the opportunity to work independently as well as with other software developers in various sprints to create full-stack applications. In collaborative settings, I made it a priority to solve for problems that would benefit the group as a whole, whether it be designing the database schema, creating frontend and backend data validations, engineering a clean and useful Redux state, or determining how our app's components would flow together via client-side routing. Needless to say, I'm all about leaving my collaborators with a strong, positive impression.

I'm excited to continue to grow as a developer, becoming more adept at solving complex challenges with a talented team in order to create flawless user experiences.

If you're wanting to chat about user-friendly UX, database design, or anything in-between, feel free to reach out!
